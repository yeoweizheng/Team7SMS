Our Teachers

Teachers enjoy building relationships between my known and my unknown from time to time (yes, this is learning too!) and feeling satisfied when the unknown becomes known to me. I wish for all our students to find this joy of learning as well when they experience our programme and teaching.



The diverse responses and engagement level from each interaction will teach students something and inspire students to make a positive impact on people in my career. Students are excited to embark on a lifelong learning journey , with our corporate spirit of Always Learning and Always Leading.


Our Stories


Our programmes are designed in consultation with industry leaders. We have internships as practice-based learning, in all our graduate programmes. These internships last between 5-months to 1 year, depending on the programmes and study format. The substantial time spent with companies ensures depth of experience and benefit for both our students and the companies they work for.



Farming Institute of Technology is able to help students and participants apply technologies, processes, methodologies, techniques and best practices because our faculty is well versed with the technology landscape as well as the international frameworks, models and standards. At the same time, we measure our results through the degree of practice deployed by individuals and companies.
