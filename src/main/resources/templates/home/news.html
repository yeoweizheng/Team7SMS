School of Sciences and Biology Produces new Vaccine

Students from School of Biology and School of Sciences come together to work on a new vaccine against flu. This break-through discovery is the first of its kind in the world. Many would believe to benefit from it.

Lounge for Undergraduates

With increase stress during the exam period, students desperately needs a place to catch-up on sleep. The student union has taken steps to emptied the equipment room near the food court to serve as a temporary lounge for students.

Pen and Paper

Studies have shown that the tradition pen and paper works best during studies. In a survey led by partner university, Kenington College, 70% of 500 students surveyed named pen and paper as their most preferred stationary of all time.
